Biography

I'm a Ph.D student in Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, working at the Novel Emerging Computing System Technologies Laboratory (NECSTLab) under the guidance of professor Marco Santambrogio. My research is centered around high-performance computing applied to graph analytics and numerical analysis, with a focus on scalable GPU computation and making heterogeneous computing more accessible to everyone. I've received my Bachelor and Master degrees at Politecnico di Milano, and studied at the Ecole Polytechnique de Bruxelles.
I like things that go fast, making things go faster, macro photography and hot enamel handicraft.
